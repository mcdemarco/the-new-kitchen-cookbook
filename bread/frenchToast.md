# French Toast

Just for the record, we cut down and munged [Alton Brown's recipe](https://www.foodnetwork.com/recipes/alton-brown/french-toast-recipe-1942216) as follows.

Serves 2.

## Ingredients

* 5 slices bread
* 2/3 c. half-and-half
* 2 eggs
* 1 T sugar
* dash salt
* dash vanilla extract
* butter for frying

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients.
2. Dunk bread.
3. Fry bread in butter.
