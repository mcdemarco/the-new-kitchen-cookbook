# Vegetables

The best thing to do with vegetables is to fry them in olive oil and lots of garlic.  If for some reason that won't do, here are some other things you can do with them.

## Cucumber Salad 

This is Peter's recipe that we brought to a potluck. Quantities are approximate.

### Directions

Mix 3 sliced cucumbers, 2/3 lb sour cream, and 4 T. wine vinegar.
